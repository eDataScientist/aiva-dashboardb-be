Scope Multi-Metric AI Conversation Grading — daily automated evaluation across 4 quality dimensions Conversations Monitoring Overhaul — dedicated route with filtering, sorting, and highlight-flagging AI Intent Classification — AI-judged classification against a fixed 16-label taxonomy Feature 1: Multi-Metric AI Conversation Grading One grade record per customer per day, produced in a single daily batch run. All numeric scores are 1–10 with AI reasoning. Replaces the current Outcome Scoring section with a new AI Quality Metrics page (aggregate trend charts + per-customer grade history). Layout: filter/sort panel, conversation list with grade indicators, conversation detail panel.
